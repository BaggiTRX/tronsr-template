## Community Support Plan

Baggi seeks to fairly distribute the rewards of the Nodes it operates.

In keeping with our core beliefs, a simple 80-10-10 model will be observed.

路 80% of all rewards will be distributed to the Baggi community registred in baggi.co in proportion to all votes cast.

路 10% of all rewards generated by Baggi shall be retained by Baggi. The use of these rewards will be determined by the community on the first of every month by a 1 vote per active member voting structure.

路 10% of all rewards must be retained by Baggi for use as operating expenses.
